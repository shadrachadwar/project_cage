Who are we?

Pass the Torch Ministries is a Christian faith based organization established with the aim of addressing vivid needs of people in communities in Uganda. Its operation is carried out with viewpoints of Empowerment for sustainable development.

Pass the Torch Ministries is an indigenous Christian faith based non-profit organization in Uganda. It was established in 2018 and incorporated in September 2019, with the aim of empowering and transforming communities in Northern Uganda. Our Ministries focus on equipping and empowering Christian Spiritual leaders, women, youth and children who are most disadvantaged within the urban poor and remote rural villages.

What we do?

The ministry equips Christian spiritual leader with skills and knowledge of servant leadership and discipleship, and materials for effective mission, we evangelize communities and reach out to the lost souls for Christ Jesus, empower young adults to meet sustainable development needs, and foster a dignified development of disadvantaged children.

PTTM believes that limitations disable individuals from moving forward effectively and efficientlty with focus and determination in given task and livelihood. In order to mitigate that limitation, we committed to do as much as we can in our capacity.

How we work

  • We organize seminars, conferences and workshops for Church leaders.
  • We also do production of training materials, and conduct informal form of education and establishing a registered training Centre (Bible College) where formal training can be carried out.
  • By providing extension services of both crop and animal husbandry expertise to the participants.
  • Motivating the farmers through Exposure-Field visit and short courses.
  • By establishing a demonstration farms for both crops and animal husbandry exposure and short trainings.
  • Including courses of agriculture and entrepreneurship in our skill development training programme.
